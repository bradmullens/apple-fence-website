> "Hi [Name], just sent over your fence quote via email. Let me know if you have any questions — happy to walk through it. — Brent, Apple Fence (512) 680-2742"

**Day 3 — Follow-up call**
- Call script: "Hi [Name], this is Brent from Apple Fence. Just wanted to check in on the quote I sent over. Did everything make sense? Any questions I can answer?"
- If voicemail: "Hey [Name], Brent from Apple Fence checking in on your quote. No pressure at all — just want to make sure you have everything you need. Call me back anytime at (512) 680-2742."

> "Hi [Name], Brent from Apple Fence. Your quote is still good if you'd like to move forward. We've got openings on the schedule this month. Just text back 'YES' and I'll get you on the books! No rush either way."
