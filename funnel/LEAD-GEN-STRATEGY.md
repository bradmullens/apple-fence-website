## Month 3: Scale & Optimize (Days 61-90)

### A/B Testing Guide

**Website tests:**
| Test | Variant A | Variant B | Metric |
|------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Hero CTA text | "Get Free Quote" | "Get My Price in 24 Hours" | Form submissions |
| CTA color | Gold (#f59e0b) | Green (#1a4731) | Click-through rate |
| Form length | Full form (7 fields) | Short form (3 fields: name, phone, service) | Completion rate |
| Trust badge | "51 Five-Star Reviews" | "Rated #1 in Leander" | Bounce rate |
| Phone number | In nav bar | Floating mobile button | Phone calls |

**Google Ads tests:**
- Ad copy: Test benefit-focused vs. urgency-focused headlines
- Landing pages: Full site vs. dedicated landing page
- Bid strategies: Manual CPC vs. Target CPA after 30+ conversions

**Process:**
1. Run each test for minimum 2 weeks or 100 conversions
2. Use Google Optimize or simple URL parameter tracking
3. Declare winner at 95% confidence
4. Implement winner, start next test

### Channel Analysis Framework

**Monthly review checklist:**

| Channel | Metrics to Track |
|---------|-----------------|
| Google Ads | Impressions, clicks, CTR, CPC, conversions, cost/conversion |
| Facebook/Instagram | Reach, engagement, link clicks, leads, cost/lead |
| Yelp | Profile views, leads, reviews gained |
| Google Business | Views, clicks, calls, direction requests |
| Nextdoor | Impressions, recommendations, leads |
| Referrals | Count, conversion rate, revenue |
| Door Hangers | Redemption rate (by promo code), revenue |
| Organic/SEO | Impressions, clicks, keyword rankings, form submissions |

**Decision framework:**
- Channel CPL < $50 → Scale budget
- Channel CPL $50-$100 → Optimize (test creative, targeting)
- Channel CPL > $100 → Reduce or cut budget
- Always compare against average job value ($2,500-$5,000)
